What is Fitbit?
The Fitbit Tracker is a wireless-enabled wearable device that measures data such as the number of steps walked, quality of sleep, and other personal metrics. Fitbit is a registered trademark and service mark of Fitbit, Inc

What is Status Board
Status Board is an awesome data visulaization iPad app developed by the talented folks at Panic

How does it work?

Fitboard connects your Fitbit to your Status Board using the Fitbit provided API. Fitbit provides your data to Fitboard using the secure, open standard OAUTH.

At anytime you can revoke Fitboard's access and remove the stored OAUTH tokens from Fitboard (your graphs will stop working). {% if session.fitbit_keys %}
